What is a mortgage calculator?

A mortgage calculator is a tool that helps you estimate the monthly payment, interest ratem and other costs associated with a mortgage. You can use a mortgage calculator to see how much you could borrow, how much interest you would pay, and how long it would take you to repay the loan.
